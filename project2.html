Horan & Fevold Hearing Clinic

Welcome to Horan & Fevold Hearing Clinic, a full-service audiology clinic with two convenient locations in Wenatchee and Moses Lake.  For more than two decades, the audiologists at Horan & Fevold have been helping the hearing impaired improve their quality of life. We understand that hearing loss impacts all functions of daily life, including our ability to communicate with family, friends, and those around us.
Our team provides trustworthy recommendations, reliable service, and affordable hearing aids, so our patients can continue to enjoy the experiences they cherish without letting communication issues slow them down.  In choosing us as their provider of better hearing, they are establishing a professional relationship that will last for many years.
We look forward to helping you on your journey to better hearing.

Our Hearing Care Services

We offer a wide array of audiological services to meet all of your hearing needs. We specialize in helping patients address their hearing difficulties with personalized solutions. Our services include hearing tests, tinnitus management, hearing aid fittings, custom hearing protection, and more.

Hearing Aids Trial & Consultation

First, our audiologist or hearing specialist does a thorough hearing test & evaluation to diagnose your hearing problem, this includes,

  • Case history
  • Otoscopy ear examination
  • Pure tone audiometry (PTA)
  • In-store hearing aids trial

At our clinic you will learn which are the best hearing aids machine in India. Based on the test results and individual’s lifestyle and budget, the audiologist will recommend what type of hearing aid style will suit you best for your hearing problem. A digital hearing aids trial is offered at the clinic and if satisfied, buy them.

Our range has a wide variety of hearing aid machine brands like Signia, Widex , GN ReSound and Starkey. You can choose among rechargeable hearing aids or battery run models basis your lifestyle and budget. We also have flexible payment options like interest free EMI to make your buying experience smooth and satisfactory.
